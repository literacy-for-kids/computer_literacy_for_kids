# Computer Literacy for Kids

Welcome to our comprehensive computer literacy curriculum designed specifically for children! This 18-week program introduces young learners to essential digital skills through fun, engaging activities.

---

## Course at a Glance

| Unit | Weeks | Topics |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Digital Foundations** | 1–4 | Internet safety, file management, digital art, search skills |
| **Programming & Creativity** | 5–7 | Scratch coding, keyboard skills, digital communication |
| **3D Design & Making** | 8–10 | TinkerCAD modeling, 3D design, print preparation |
| **AI & Digital Citizenship** | 11–12 | AI basics, cyber safety, responsible online behavior |
| **Multimedia & Data** | 13–15 | Presentations, video editing, spreadsheets & charts |
| **Final Project** | 16–18 | Plan, develop, and showcase a creative digital project |

---

## What Each Week Includes

Every week follows a consistent three-session structure:

- **Guided Session 1** — Introduce new concepts with hands-on activities
- **Guided Session 2** — Deepen learning and build on Session 1
- **Independent Session** — 20 minutes of self-directed practice to reinforce skills

Each session also includes:
- Clear learning objectives
- Step-by-step activity instructions
- Reflection questions
- Teacher preparation checklists
